# Mirage

**Idea:** The page at [mirage.web.cpctf.space](http://mirage.web.cpctf.space/) lists many brace-strings that look like flags but use **wrong prefixes** (`TGT7D`, `CPCLD`, `CNCKF`, …) or are obvious jokes. Only one line uses the real contest prefix **`CPCTF{...}`**.

**Solve:** Pick the single **`CPCTF{...}`** entry and submit it (copy/paste).

**Flag:** `CPCTF{CH4R4C73R5_4L50_D159U153}`
